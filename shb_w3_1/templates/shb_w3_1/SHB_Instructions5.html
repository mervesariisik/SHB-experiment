Guesses

The Guessers can enter their GUESS of the secret number in 0.5 increments: 5, 5.5, 6, 6.5, 7, 7.5, 8, 8.5, 9, 9.5, 10

How are payoffs determined?

The payoffs depend on the GUESS such that:

  • the Observer earns more when the GUESS is higher

  • the Guesser earns more when the GUESS is closer to the secret number

The payoffs of this game are defined in terms of points as displayed in the tables below.

For example, suppose GUESS is 6.5

  • the Observer gets 23 points
    (regardless of the secret number)

  • if the secret number is 6, the Guesser gets 104 points
  • if the secret number is 10, the Guesser gets 23 points

Payoff tables will also be available on your decision screens.

Your final earning from this part ("Guessing the Secret Number") will be calculated by converting the TOTAL points you collect throughout the {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds into US dollars. The rate at which points are worth US dollars will be shown on the screen before the game begins.
