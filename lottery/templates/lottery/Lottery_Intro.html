You will now be presented with several Situations, each asking you to make a choice between two options:

  1. 50-50 Lottery between 40 points or 0 points (each equally likely)
    2. or
  2. Fixed Payoff of a specific number of points

When you have made all your choices, the computer will randomly select one Situation to determine your earning from this part.

In the selected Situation: if you chose the 50-50 Lottery, the computer will randomly pick 40 points or 0 points and if you chose the Fixed Payoff, you will receive the specified number of points.

For every point you earned in the selected Situation, you will get $0.05. Your earnings from this part will be added to your fixed reward and your earnings from other parts.

Note that you will have up to 3 minutes to submit all your choices. In the case that you don't submit your choices within the allotted time, the computer will submit random choices.
